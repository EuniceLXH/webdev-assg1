What Is Floorball?

It is a type of floor hockey with 5 players and a goalkeeper in each team.
Matches are played 20 minutes per period, with a total of 3 periods.

How to hold a floorball stick?

Rules for Floorball

    It is not allowed to:
  • hit,block,lift or kick an opponent's stick
  • push,tackle or obstruct the opponent
  • play the ball above the knee level with the stick
  • raise the blade above waist level when hitting the ball
  • jump up and stop the ball
  • play the ball with your stick between the opponent's legs

World Tournaments

The biggest floorball tournament is the World Floorball Championship
